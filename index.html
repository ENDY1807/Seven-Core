The Great Archmage's Prophecy

In the mystical lands of Aetheria, seven ancient magic cores have been discovered, each representing a different school of arcane power.

The Seven Core server invites you to embark on an epic journey through enchanted forests, floating islands, and forgotten ruins. Prove your worth as a mage, collect rare artifacts, and master the seven schools of magic.
